About GOLO SA

The home of DISKI. We bring all the coverage in a more defined view.

GOLO SA was founded on the idea of empowering football fans ,players and coaches with more knowledge and analyisis about the game we all love and live for. GOLO SA covers all South African proffesional divisions ,we showcase the skills ,the goals ,the saves ,the tackles ,the drama ,the fights ,the stats ,the rising stars ,the best vs the best ,the coach-talk ,the analysis ,etc. And whats more fascinating is what all those come with ,we dont just entertain - we also teach . We offer a more insightful eye into the game. We ensure you can see more than you see when watching the game live. We offer you the chance to understand lines in between and give you the feel of been in control.
